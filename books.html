Read more about history
  • LEONARDO DE VINCI- Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) – A Renaissance genius, known for The Mona Lisa and The Last Supper, as well as his innovations in science and engineering.
  • Michelangelo- Michelangelo (1475–1564) – A master sculptor and painter, famous for the Sistine Chapel Ceiling and David.
  • Claude Monet- Claude Monet (1840–1926) – A founder of Impressionism, known for Water Lilies and Impression, Sunrise.
  • Vincent van Gogh-  Vincent van Gogh (1853–1890) – A post-impressionist painter known for Starry Night and Sunflowers, whose emotional intensity shaped modern art.
  • Pablo_picasso- Pablo Picasso (1881–1973) – A pioneer of Cubism, famous for Guernica and Les Demoiselles d’Avignon
  • Rembrandt van Rijn- Rembrandt van Rijn (1606–1669) – A Dutch master known for his dramatic use of light and shadow in paintings like The Night Watch.
  • Salvador Dalí- Salvador Dalí (1904–1989) – A surrealist icon, famous for The Persistence of Memory and his dreamlike imagery.
